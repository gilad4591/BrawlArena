About BrawlArena

Brawl Arena is a fast, arcade-style beat-'em-up where a roster of distinct fighters — each with three signature special moves — battle across a set of hand-built arenas. It runs in the browser and as a mobile app, with touch controls on phones/tablets and full keyboard support on desktop.

▶ Play now

Game modes

  • Solo Campaign — five escalating stages against bruisers, mages and a boss.
  • Arcade — 1v1, free-for-all against up to 8 CPU opponents, or teams (with a one-tap random shuffle).
  • Online Multiplayer — invite a friend with a 6-digit code and fight cross-device in free-for-all or teams.

How to play

  • Move with the joystick (or arrow keys / WASD on desktop). Up and down shift your depth on the floor.
  • HIT for light melee combos, Jump to hop projectiles, DEF to guard.
  • Specials: tap SP for your neutral move; double-tap forward + SP for a dash attack; jump + SP for an air launcher.
  • Pick up weapons and props from the floor to throw or swing a few times.

Progression

Win matches to earn XP and unlock the full roster. Everything you unlock is saved on your device.
