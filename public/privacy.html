BrawlArena — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

Brawl Arena ("the Game") is a browser and mobile fighting game. This policy explains what information the Game handles. We designed it to collect as little as possible.

Information we store

The Game does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email, or any personal identifier. Your gameplay preferences — settings, statistics, unlocked characters, campaign progress, and a display name you optionally type — are saved locally on your own device (browser local storage / app preferences). This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us.

Multiplayer

When you play online, the Game connects to a relay server only to pass match messages (positions, inputs) between you and the other players in your room, identified by a temporary invite code. These messages are relayed in real time and are not stored or logged.

Advertising

The Game shows ads through Google (AdSense on the web, AdMob in the mobile app). Google and its partners may use cookies or device identifiers to serve and measure ads, including personalised ads where permitted. You can learn about and control this at Google Ads & Privacy and, on the web, at aboutads.info/choices.

Children

The Game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

You can clear all locally-stored progress at any time from the in-game Settings ("Reset Progress"), or by clearing your browser/app data.
